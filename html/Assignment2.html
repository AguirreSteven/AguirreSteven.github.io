The Strokes

I recently started listening to The Strokes discography these last couple of weeks. I always knew they existed, but I never gave all of their albums a chance. I made a little list of some of my favorites songs across each album but overall they are a great band. The lead singer, Julian Casablancas has a sort of tone in his music that is reminicent of mid 90s rock and it is exceptional. Some of my favorite songs so far are Ize of the World and The Adults Are Talking.
